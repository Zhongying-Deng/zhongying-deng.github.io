About Me (Google Scholar)

I am a post-doctoral research associate at the (DAMTP), University of Cambridge, working with Dr Angelica I. Aviles-Rivero, Prof. Carola-Bibiane Schönlieb, and Prof. Zoe Kourtzi. I received my doctor's degree from the University of Surrey, UK, supervised by Professor Tao Xiang and Professor Yi-Zhe Song. During my PhD years, I took internships at the University of Cambridge, Shanghai AI Lab, and iFlyTek. I received my master's degree from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) and Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology (SIAT), Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS), supervised by Professor Yu Qiao and Doctor Xiaojiang Peng. I am also fortunate to work with Professor Zhifeng Li from 2016 to 2017.
